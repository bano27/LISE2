LISE PLATFORM


Through LISE we are looking to indulge the art society in our community to come out and share their art through our platform.

Our platform offers to give individual artist their own profile upon registeration to LISE (artist's can find the registeration form on our home section ) where they can post their recent or earlier work to attract clients and interact with their interested clients

Clients/Users will be able visit our buy section incase they are interested in purchasing art from one of our artist and have the priviledge to scroll through various artists and find those whom they feel attached to their work.
